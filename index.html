About

Erma Swartz is an environmental advocate and versatile professional with a robust background in data analysis, digital strategy, and sustainability-focused initiatives. Currently pursuing a Master of Science at Columbia University, her work centers on analyzing waste systems, urban resilience, and circular economy strategies. Her career spans diverse roles, including managing digital campaigns, optimizing data processes, and producing impactful policy reports.

Professionally, Erma has worked in the nonprofit and political sectors, leading data-driven campaigns and crafting actionable insights for stakeholders. Her expertise in GIS, technical writing, and collaboration has supported her success in addressing complex challenges across various fields. Beyond her professional pursuits, she is a certified yoga instructor, an experienced diver, and an avid environmental steward, merging her passions for sustainability and community-focused solutions.
