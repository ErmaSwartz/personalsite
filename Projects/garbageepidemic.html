The Garbage Epidemic

Exploring the impact of waste mismanagement and how it shapes urban landscapes.

Project Overview

This project analyzes the success of the Zero Waste Policy in New York City. It takes into account economic, spatial and social relationships in order to dive deeper into what may be working or failing within the cities 2015 policy.
